Privacy & Policy - SKYWAY

Welcome to SKYWAY, your trusted ticket reservation platform. Below, you will find comprehensive details about how we manage your personal data in compliance with RODO (General Data Protection Regulation).

Who is responsible for your data?
The data controller is SKYWAY Ltd., located at 123 Sky Avenue, Cloud City, Country.

What data do we collect?
We collect the following personal data:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Payment details
  • Reservation preferences

How do we use your data?
We use your data to:

  • Process your ticket reservations
  • Provide personalized customer support
  • Send updates and reminders about your bookings
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

How long do we retain your data?
Your personal data is retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined above, or as required by law. Typically, this means:

  • Transaction data is retained for up to 7 years for audit and compliance purposes.
  • Contact data is retained until you request its deletion.

What are your rights?
Under RODO, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct or update inaccurate data
  • Request the deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to data processing
  • Data portability (transfer your data to another provider)
To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@skyway.com.

How do we protect your data?
We implement strict technical and organizational measures to ensure your data is secure, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive information
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls to limit who can access your data

For further inquiries, please contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@skyway.com.
